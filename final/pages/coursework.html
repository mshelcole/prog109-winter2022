Coursework

PROG 109 introduces fundamental programming and mark-up techniques for the Web. Students design procedures and write computer instructions to solve business related problems with a focus on simple applications and games. Students learn procedural programming, develop user interfaces, and work with events and objects. The class will teach client-side web development fundamentals.

This is a 5 credit class, Instructed by Maricel Medina

Mondays & Wednesdays from 10:30AM-12:20PM in room N252


DMA 107: WEB MULTIMEDIA FUNDAMENTALS is a core course for the Digital Media Arts program, DMA 107 provides a hands-on overview of the design, maintenance and use of web sites, particularly as they are used to do business in the digital media industry sector. Students create simple Web sites by applying basic design principles, coding languages and industry standards while taking into consideration delivery platform-specific needs. This 5 credit Course met Online only, Instructed by Janet Eileen
