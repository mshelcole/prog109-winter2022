About Me

Michelle Coleman is a currently Product Manager and part-time student at Bellevue College. She considers herself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on her academic foundations in psychology and sociology and stay in tune with the latest technology strategies through continued coursework.

Over the years, Michelle has been involved in several large integration projects, working across global team interlocks to gather requirements, align testing, assist with business enablement, and post-deployment support. Most recently, Michelle is a Product Manager, working closely with business stakeholders to define the roadmap and deliver key capabilities to improve the sales experience.

Michelle believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success - a tenet she lives out through her interests in yoga, meditation, and gardening. Michelle is currently working as a Salesforce Product Manager and is always interested in a new challenge. Reach out to michellecoleman@gmail.com to connect!
